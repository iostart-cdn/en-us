Advanced Security Features

Cold Storage

Your private keys are never exposed to the internet, which means they are protected from online threats such as phishing and malware attacks.

Passphrase Protection

For an added layer of security, Trézor allows you to use a passphrase along with your recovery seed.

Secure Transaction Signing

Each time you authorize a transaction, the Trézor device requires your physical confirmation.

Anti-Phishing Features

Trézor Suite includes anti-phishing measures to prevent any phishing attempts.
